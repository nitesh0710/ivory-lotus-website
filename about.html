About Us

Mission: Ivory Lotus Design Studio is committed to creating thoughtful interiors that balance contemporary design with India’s rich cultural heritage. We aim to craft functional, sustainable and beautiful spaces that reflect each client’s personality and honour the environment.

Vision: To become a leading Indian design studio recognised for timeless interiors that celebrate heritage and modernity. We strive to inspire a global appreciation for Indian design sensibilities while raising the bar for quality, craftsmanship and client care.
